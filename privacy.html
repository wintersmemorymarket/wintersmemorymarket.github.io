Privacy Policy

Winters Memory Market LLC · Last updated: May 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Winters Memory Market LLC ("we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with our internal business tools and any integrations with third-party platforms including Pinterest, eBay, Etsy, Facebook, and Instagram.

1. Who We Are

Winters Memory Market LLC is a small vintage and antique reselling business operating in Pinehurst, NC and Palm Bay, FL. Our internal tools are used solely to manage inventory, generate product listings, and schedule social media posts for our own business use.

2. Information We Collect

Our internal tools collect and store the following types of information:

Inventory data: Item names, descriptions, costs, sale prices, photos, and related business records stored in our private Firebase database.

API credentials: Anthropic API keys entered by authorized users are stored locally in the user's browser and are never transmitted to or stored on our servers.

Platform access tokens: OAuth tokens used to post content to Pinterest and other platforms on behalf of our business accounts. These are stored securely and used only to publish content we have created.

3. How We Use Information

All data collected by our tools is used exclusively for the following internal business purposes:

· Managing our product inventory and sales records
· Generating product listing descriptions and social media captions
· Scheduling and publishing posts to our own social media accounts
· Tracking campaign performance for our own listings

We do not sell, share, rent, or otherwise disclose any data to third parties for marketing or any other purposes.

4. Third-Party Platforms

Our tools integrate with the following platforms solely to publish content on our own business accounts:

· Pinterest: Used to publish pins of our inventory to our business boards
· eBay: Used to manage and publish product listings
· Etsy: Used to manage and publish product listings
· Facebook: Used to publish posts to relevant marketplace groups
· Instagram: Used to publish product photos and captions

Each platform's use of data is governed by their respective privacy policies. We access only the permissions necessary to publish content on our own accounts.

5. Data Storage and Security

Business data is stored in Google Firebase, a secure cloud database service. Access is restricted to authorized members of Winters Memory Market LLC. We do not store customer data, payment information, or personal information about third parties.

6. Cookies

Our internal tools may use browser localStorage to remember user preferences such as API keys on trusted devices. No tracking cookies or analytics cookies are used.

7. Children's Privacy

Our tools and services are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of our tools constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
